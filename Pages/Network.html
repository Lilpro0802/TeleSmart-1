Our Network
Redundant, High-Performance Network
Deliver high-performance bandwidth
Smartware Technologies network is built on our resilient fibre backbone, with Nx100G capacity, and is engineered to deliver high-performance bandwidth when and where you need it. Our footprint includes the lowest-latency transatlantic network and one of largest fibre optic networks.
Reliable, high-quality traffic delivery
We run multiple, diverse and redundant fibre paths around the India, ensuring reliable, high-quality traffic delivery even if a fibre route is compromised. Our IP network includes carrier-class routing infrastructure and has the capacity to transport over 14 Tbps of traffic at any given time.
Monitor network capacity and performance
Our Traffic Engineering ensures that your traffic takes the most direct, lowest-latency path available, from origination to destination. We monitor network capacity and performance, and proactively upgrade our infrastructure, to ensure seamless delivery of mission-critical traffic, even as customer bandwidth requirements grow.
